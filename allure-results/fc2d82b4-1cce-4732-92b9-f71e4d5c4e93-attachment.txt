Title: Pravin Kumari defies odds at Khelo India Beach Games
Text: Footballer Pravin Kumari from Himachal Pradesh showcased exceptional grit and resilience at the Khelo India Beach Games 2026, inspiring a remarkable comeback win despite playing through a painful leg injury. Battling deep-rooted social barriers and limited resources, she has emerged as a powerful symbol of determination, leadership, and the steady rise of women's football from rural India.
